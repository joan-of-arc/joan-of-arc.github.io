How to Play Quantum Chess

Classical Moves: Select a piece, then select destination.

Quantum Split: Select a piece, enable split, then select two destinations.

Entanglement: Select two quantum pieces to entangle them.

Collapse: Resolve all quantum states to classical positions.

Note: You play both sides to explore quantum mechanics.
